Common Questions

Got questions? We have answers.

Do I need to download an app? ▾
No. GroupYetu360 is a Progressive Web App — it works in any browser on any phone, tablet or computer. You can also add it to your home screen for a native app-like experience. An Android app is also available on the Google Play Store.
How does the M-Pesa integration work? ▾
On Standard and Pro plans, members can pay directly via M-Pesa STK Push from their group portal. The payment prompt goes to their phone, they enter their PIN, and the contribution is recorded automatically. We support Paybill, Till Number and Send Money. Your M-Pesa PIN is entered directly with Safaricom — GroupYetu360 never sees it.
How does Bulk SMS work? ▾
Bulk SMS is available on all plans on a pay-as-you-go basis. You purchase SMS credits in bundles from within the platform — powered by our SMS partners Celcom Africa and SMS Leopard. Credits do not expire. Admins can send meeting reminders, contribution notices and welfare updates to all members in one click.
Is my group's financial data safe? ▾
Yes. All data is stored on Supabase infrastructure (AWS, SOC 2 Type 2 certified) with encryption at rest and in transit. Row-level security ensures each organisation can only ever see its own data. We comply with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.
Can members see each other's balances? ▾
No. Each member can only see their own personal records — their contributions, balances and welfare history. Admins and treasurers have access to the full group view. You control exactly who sees what.
What happens when my free trial ends? ▾
After the 2-month free trial, your account reverts to Starter plan limits if you do not subscribe. Your data is retained for 90 days after expiry. There is no auto-charge — you pay only when you choose to. You will receive reminders before your trial ends.
Can I use GroupYetu360 for a church or sports club? ▾
Absolutely. GroupYetu360 is designed for any community group that collects money, tracks members and holds meetings — chamas, welfare groups, investment clubs, churches, SACCOs, football clubs, women's groups, neighbourhood associations and more. If your group has members and money, GroupYetu360 works for you.
