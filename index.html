Everything you need

One platform. All your group needs.

No more WhatsApp spreadsheets and manual follow-ups. GroupYetu360 brings your entire group operation into one professional platform.

₭
Finance & Contributions
Track member savings, shares and expenses. See the full financial picture — bank balance, member balances and admin funds — in real time.
◉
Member Management
Full member register with status tracking, registration records, balance history and a personal member portal each member can log into.
📱
M-Pesa Integration
Members pay via STK Push directly from their phone. Payments are recorded automatically. Supports Paybill, Till and Send Money.
✉
Bulk SMS
Send meeting reminders, contribution notices and welfare updates to all members in one click via Africa's Talking.
♡
Welfare Management
Create welfare events for bereavements, community contributions and celebrations. Track who has contributed and automatically calculate per-member amounts.
📅
Meetings & Attendance
Schedule meetings, send SMS reminders and take attendance digitally. Track each member's attendance rate over time.
⚑
Projects & Investments
Track farms, rental properties, businesses and investments. See acquisition costs, income and ROI for each project.
🔐
Roles & Security
Admin, treasurer, officer and member roles each see only what they need. Members get their own portal to view their balance and contribution history.
🌍
Diaspora Ready
Manage your group from anywhere in the world. Works on any device — phone, tablet or computer — with no app download required.
