Built specifically for
Kenyan community groups

Every feature was designed around how chamas, welfare associations and investment clubs actually operate in Kenya.

👥
Member Management
Register members, track registration fees, manage shares and savings balances. Members get their own portal to view their contributions and attend meetings.
💰
Financial Records
Record contributions, expenses and income. Track every M-Pesa payment with reference numbers. Get a real-time net position of your group's finances.
📱
Bulk SMS
Send meeting reminders, contribution notices and announcements to all members at once. Works on Safaricom, Airtel and Telkom numbers.
📅
Meeting Management
Schedule meetings, record attendance and track who was present, apologised or absent. Automatic SMS reminders before each meeting.
❤️
Welfare Tracking
Record welfare events and track contributions per member. Configurable rates for member death, spouse/parent and child events.
🏗️
Projects & Investments
Track your group's investments and projects — farms, rental properties, businesses. Link expenses directly to each project.
🔒
Role-Based Access
Assign Admin, Treasurer, Officer or Member roles. Treasurer-only debit/credit, activity audit trail, and optional 2FA security.
📊
Activity Audit Log
Every sensitive action is logged — who debited, credited or deleted what and when. Resolve disputes with a complete trail of admin activity.
📲
Works on Any Device
Install on your phone like a native app, or use on any computer browser. No download required — works instantly from any device, anywhere in the world.
🌍
Built for Diaspora Groups
Manage your Kenyan community group from anywhere in the world. Members in Nairobi, Kigali, London or Toronto all access the same platform with a unique org code login.
⭐
Custom Features on Premium
Premium plan clients get custom features built specifically for their organisation's needs. Unique workflows, reports, or integrations — we build it for you.
